Simple Cryptogram

Mistakes: 0/3

How to play

Each puzzle is a famous quote in secret code. Every letter A–Z has been swapped for a different letter. Your job is to figure out the original quote.

Reading the puzzle

  • Top — encrypted letter (what you see in the clue).
  • Middle — your guess for that letter.
  • Bottom — number shared by every box with the same encrypted letter.

Punctuation and spaces are already correct and cannot be selected.

Making a guess

  1. Tap any letter box in the quote (all matching numbers highlight).
  2. Tap a letter on the keyboard to assign it to that number.
  3. Tap Clear to remove your guess for the selected number.

Each plaintext letter A–Z can only be used once. If a letter is already assigned elsewhere, that key is disabled until you clear it.

Givens, mistakes, and difficulty

  • White guesses are given at the start and cannot be changed.
  • You have three mistakes. A wrong guess counts once per number; after three, the puzzle ends — use Reset to try the same quote again.
  • Easy reveals many letters; Expert reveals only one starter letter. Change difficulty before starting a New Game.

Solve every number correctly to win. Your progress is saved automatically.
